Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how you use our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for website functionality (booking system, secure areas) Session
Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website 1 year
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings 1 year
Marketing Cookies Track visitors across websites for advertising purposes 1 year

3. Specific Cookies We Use

Stripe Payment Cookies: Necessary for processing secure payments

Session Cookies: Maintain your booking session

Preference Cookies: Remember your language and currency preferences

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Maps: Location services in booking form

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies
  • Cookie Preferences: Use our cookie consent banner to manage preferences
  • Third-Party Tools: Browser extensions for cookie management

6. Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data

Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data

Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data

Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect website functionality:

  • Booking system may not work properly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some features may be unavailable

8. Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
